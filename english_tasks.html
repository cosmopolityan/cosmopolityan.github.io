EXERCISES (A)

Ex. 1

I. Write short forms (she's/we're, etc.).

1. he is = he's

2. they are = they're

3. she is not = she isn't

4. it is not = it isn't

5. I am not = I'm not

6. You are not = You're not

II. Write the full forms (she's/we're, etc.).

7. we aren't = we are not

8. I'm = I am

9. you're = you are

10. they aren't = they are not

11. it isn't = it is not

12. she's = she is
